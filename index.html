What I Do

Services I provide for my clients

User Research

Conducting user research to gather insights into users' behaviors, preferences, and needs to inform the design of a digital product.

User Interface

Designing the visual and interactive elements of a digital product, such as icons, buttons, menus, and color schemes.

User Experience

I Create user-centered designs that optimize the overall experience of a digital product, including its usability, accessibility, and satisfaction.

Information Architecture

Organizing the content and structure of a digital product to ensure users can easily find what they need and complete their desired tasks.

Interaction Design

Defining how users interact with a digital product, including the flow of information and actions they take to accomplish their goals.

Prototyping

Creating prototypes and conducting testing with users to validate design concepts and iterate on the product design.
